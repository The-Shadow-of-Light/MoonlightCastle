Frequently Asked Questions

Moonlight Castle refers both to the name of my webnovel Moonlight Castle and to the castle itself.

1 = The name of my webnovel Moonlight Castle

2 = Moonlight Castle itself is a magic castle that travels from world to world. It is fueled by moonlight, as such it travels to worlds which has it.

Kazimir is the protagonist of Moonlight Castle. He grew up a poor street rat and has learned to survive by himself.

The Eternal Dance of Light and Darkness is the series of novels I have written, they all revolve around Selis Everlight and Kazimir, about how their souls are connected, about how they are destined to meet each other in every universe. Moonlight Castle is the first novel I wrote in this series.

You can read my novels in any order you wish. I suggest just looking through them and choosing the one you find most interesting.

I have always enjoyed romance ever since I was a kid, but there were always things I disliked about it such as love triangles, harems, cheating etc. Later on I realized what soulmate romance was and I kinda fell in love with the concept. That is one of the main reasons why I started writing The Eternal Dance of Light and Darkness.

I started writing bc I could never find a novel I truly enjoyed reading. Don't get me wrong, a lot of novels did come close to it, but even novels that came close to it had certain things that just weren't to my taste. As such I decided to just try and write my own novel. One I could truly enjoy reading.

The idea for Moonlight Castle came to me when I was trying to sleep but couldn't. So as per usual I just started fantasizing about stuff, about distant fantasy worlds. Then I thought to myself, if I could live any life I could think of, what kind of life would I want to live? I decided that living inside of Moonlight Castle would be my perfect life.

Moonlight Castle will forever be free to read. I heard that if you want to publish your writing physically you can't have it online for free, but I don't expect my writing to be popular enough to be published, and even if it did I will make sure you can read Moonlight Castle for free online.

I grew up broke myself so I know how it feels like to want to read but can't afford to. As a little kid I always enjoyed reading, but at the library they only had old books for adults without any drawings etc. So I used to go to a bookstore and read the nice children's books there. It took quite some passive aggressiveness for my dumb child brain to finally figure out you weren't supposed to do that. I didn't really have access to the internet back then, so I couldn't read it online for free through other means.

For legal reasons I can't tell you how to read books online for free. However if you type "FMHY" into google you might, theoretically, find a lot of nice things to read online. Idk tho, I am dumb asf. So don't hold me responsible for anything. Remember to use tor, a proxy or a vpn or whatever. And always be careful for malware. This is just advice from one broke boy to another.

So yeah, I will do my best to make sure my writing will always be able to be read online for free.
